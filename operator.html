Operators in Python

What are Operators?

Operators are special symbols in Python that carry out arithmetic or logical computation . The value that the operator operates on is called the operand.
For eg:1+2=3 . Here + is an operator that conducts an operation on the two operands.
There are many types of operators but we will only talk about 4 types.
