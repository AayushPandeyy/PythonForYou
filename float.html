Float Data Types

What are Floats?

Float stands for Floating Point Numbers.They represent real numbers and are written with a decimal point dividing the integer and fractional parts. Floats may also be in scientific notation, with E or e indicating the power of 10 (2.5e2 = 2.5 x 102 = 250).

How to assign a float to a Variable.

Assigning float to a variable is same as assigning integers.

Syntax : Variable_name=Float_value

All of the above Variables store an Floating Point Numbers.
